## About Me

- 🖖 Hi! I am a **Ph.D. candidate** at [Kristiania University College](https://www.kristiania.no){:target="_blank"}, Oslo, Norway.

- 🔬 I love doing experimental research and writing secure code to explore new ideas while making a positive impact through my contributions. I value curiosity, humility, and humor very highly.

- 🎓 I have a background in software engineering, software security, and automated software testing.

- 🦾 My recent research interest is in _artificial intelligence_. One day, we might worship AI (until we get rid of periaqueductal grey), and I want the humanity to have the right AI to worship.

- 🎮 Apart from computer science and artificial intelligence, I have a bunch of hobbies to keep me distracted from my main goal (which I don't know yet). I love to learn new languages (both human and programming), play guitar, play computer games (I'll release my own someday), practice photography, and do occasional watercolor paintings.
